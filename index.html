Benefits of Menstrual Cycle Charting

Body Literacy

Referred to as the 5th vital sign, the menstrual cycle can provide deep insights into a person's overall health. Knowing how to interpret what your body is telling you through menstrual cycle charting, having body literacy1, can be extremely useful when making healthcare decisions. Having body literacy also helps people navigate normal cycle fluctuations.2

  • 1. Matus, G. and Wershler, L. (2005). Society for Menstrual Research Conference

    2. Migeon, A., 2017

Natural Birth Control

Symptothermal fertility awareness based methods, such as the Justisse method, taught by a qualified teacher, like me, are 99.6% effective3 at preventing pregnancy with perfect use.

  • 3. Frank-Herrmann et al., 2007

Optimizing Conception

Learning to identify your fertile window is critical for optimizing conception. One study following patients at assisted reproductive clinics found that while 87% of patients experiencing infertility had actively tried to improve their knowledge of fertility, 68% believed they were getting the timing right, but only 13% had a good understanding of the fertile window.4

  • 4. Hamton, Mazza, and Newton, 2012
