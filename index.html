Optimizing Conception

Learning to identify your fertile window is critical for optimizing conception. One study following patients at assisted reproductive clinics found that while 87% of patients experiencing infertility had actively tried to improve their knowledge of fertility, 68% believed they were getting the timing right, but only 13% had a good understanding of the fertile window.2
